DIY-PS

For this project, I had to come up with basic version of photoshop.

By doing that, I had to create 10 different brushes.

During the process, I had to go through the notes to see try out different types of line and such.

Overall, I had fun doing this project!

Keyboard brushes:

1 - white thin line / 2 - pink thin line / 3 - orange thin line / 4 - blue thin line / 5 - white chalk line

v - purple chalk / i - light blue chalk / p - pink chalk / w - white chalk / y - yellow chalk

g - white rabbit stamp
