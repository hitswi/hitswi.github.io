Net Art

The prompt for this project was to create atleast 10 pages of series (topic / story / experience) using a program called github.

For mine, I did a project based on personal experience but more on someone's point of view.

A lot of times, personally, it's easier to express their feelings rather than ourselves feelings.

I like tragic love story because it relates to me a lot and for others too.

When it comes to having best friend, whether if it's the same sex or opposite, sometimes you felt like you want them so much that you start to feel greedy and just wanting them for yourself.

However, that desire of getting someone you love cannot be pursue because of different reasons. Thus, this become one-sided love and it becomes painful for others.

I felt like doing this type of story reveals the reality of love.
